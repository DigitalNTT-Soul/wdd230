Site Name: Knoxville, Tennessee Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

Based on the name of the city in which the chamber operates.

Site Purpose:

This organization is made up of local business leaders who seek to cooperatively and mutually support and promote one another and to develop good policies and better communities to make Knoxville the best city there is! This site intends to serve as an information center for those business leaders and those interested in their activities.

Scenarios:

  • How can I contact the Chamber's Board of Directors?
  • What public activities are being held in the area by the Chamber or its members?
  • How can I join the Chamber?
  • How can I which other local business leaders have joined the Chamber?

Color Schema:

Standard White - ffffff

University of Tennessee Orange - ff8200

Smokey Gray - #58595b

Typography:

"Playfair Display" is the font that will generally go in main body content

The font being used for the main headings in Orange is called "Permanent Marker"

Subordinate headings (such as the labels for the color schema above) will be in "Roboto" font.
